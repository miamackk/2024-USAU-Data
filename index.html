USAU College Women's D1 2024 Season

USA Ultimate keeps detailed records of each team's season performance, including not just data about every game played, but also archived data for team rankings at different points throughout the season. This is a review of the spring 2024 Ultimate Frisbee season for the Division 1 College Women's league.

Power rating is a numerical value assigned to a team that represents its overall strength. It is determined by a formula which was made to ensure that teams are ranked based on ability rather than by wins alone, encouraging teams to play other teams of similar strength to improve their rating. This metric is what helps to determine which regions receive additional bids to the national tournament. A team's power rating is calculated after every tournament, averaging their performance for each game.

Ultimate frisbee games can end in one of two ways:

  1. If a team scores 15 points, they are the winner
  2. 90 minutes after the start of the game, "soft cap" goes off. Finish the current point, and now the game is to 1 + the highest score. So, if soft cap occurs and the score is 12 - 11, you add 1 to the highest score, 12 + 1 = 13, and now the score to win is 13. The first team to 13 wins.
